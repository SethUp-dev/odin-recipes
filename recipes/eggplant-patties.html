Eggplant Patties

Description

Eggplant patties are budget-friendly and healthy eggplant recipe. It is simple and made of ingredients that are just right in your alley. If you are someone who wants to eat healthy but doesn't like veggies, then this recipe is perfect for you.

Ingredients

  • 2-3 eggplants
  • 1 spring onion (chopped)
  • 1 medium onion (minced)
  • 2 cloves garlic (minced)
  • 1/4 bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs
  • parmesan cheese
  • paprika seasoning
  • basil seasoning
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • oil for frying

Steps

  1. Boil or grill the eggplants. Peel while still how to remove the skin easier.
  2. After peeling, cut in half and remove the dark seeds (if there's any). Then chop the eggplant into small pieces (mince or mash).
  3. In a mixing bowl, combine the mixed eggplant, spring onion, garlic, bread crumbs, eggs, parmesan cheese. Mix well. Season with paprika, basil, salt and pepper. Set aside.
  4. In a pan, heat some oil for frying. Scoop aroung 1/4 cup of the eggplant mixture. Fry until golden brown. Flip halfway to cook the other side. Fry the rest of the mixture.
  5. Serve with your favorite dip.
